Refund Policy

Last updated: July 2026

1. Overview

At Pring, customer satisfaction is our priority. This Refund Policy explains when and how refunds are issued for orders placed through our app.

2. Eligible Situations for Refund

  • An item was missing, damaged, expired or significantly different from what was ordered.
  • An order was cancelled by Pring due to stock unavailability after payment was made.
  • Duplicate payment or a payment failure that still deducted money from your account.
  • Delivery was not completed within a reasonable time and the customer chooses to cancel.

3. How to Request a Refund

  1. Open the "Orders" section in the Pring app and select the relevant order.
  2. Tap "Report an Issue" and choose the reason that applies to your case.
  3. Attach a photo if the issue relates to a damaged or incorrect item.
  4. Submit the request — our support team typically responds within 24 hours.

4. Refund Timeline

  • Approved refunds to Pring Wallet are credited instantly.
  • Refunds to the original payment method (UPI, card, wallet) take 3–7 business days depending on your bank.
  • Cash on Delivery refunds are issued as Pring Wallet credit or bank transfer upon verification.

5. Non-Refundable Cases

  • Change of mind after the order has been packed or dispatched.
  • Issues reported more than 24 hours after delivery without valid reason.
  • Perishable items correctly delivered in good condition as ordered.

6. Cancellations

Orders can be cancelled free of charge before they are packed by the store. Once an order is out for delivery, cancellation may not be possible, but you may still report an issue after delivery if applicable.

7. Contact for Refund Support

For any refund-related queries, reach out via the in-app Help Center or email support@pring-app.example with your order ID.
